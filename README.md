# Desk Power Controller & Enclosure

A compact, 3D-printed mounting solution designed for efficient power management at your desk. This project features a custom enclosure for a power switch and a current limiter, keeping your workspace organized, safe, and clutter-free.

## Features
* **Custom Enclosure:** Sleek, low-profile housing for your power switch and current limiter.
* **Desk-Mounted:** Secure, ergonomic design for easy access without taking up valuable workspace.
* **Cable Management:** Optimized for clean routing to keep cables hidden and tidy.
* **3D Print Ready:** Clean, manifold topology (quad-based) for reliable, high-quality printing.

## Technical Details
* **Software:** Designed in Blender (100% Quad-based topology for structural integrity).
* **Components:** 
    * **Cam Switch:** LW26 cam switch
    * **Inrush Current Limiter:** Mean Well ICL-16
* **Setup:** Ready for mounting under your desk.
